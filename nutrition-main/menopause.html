Menopause Nutrition

Supporting Health and Well-being During Menopause

Get Menopause Plan

Key Nutrients for Menopause:

  • Calcium: Helps prevent bone loss (osteoporosis).
  • Vitamin D: Aids calcium absorption.
  • Magnesium: Supports bone health and sleep.
  • Omega-3s: May help manage mood and heart health.
  • Fiber: Promotes gut health and regulates blood sugar.

Food to Include:

  • Dairy products (milk, yogurt, cheese)
  • Leafy green vegetables
  • Fatty fish (salmon, tuna)
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Whole grains

Tips for Managing Menopause Symptoms:

  • Stay hydrated to manage hot flashes.
  • Eat a balanced diet to maintain a healthy weight.
  • Engage in regular exercise to boost mood and bone health.
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol, which can trigger hot flashes.
  • Consider plant-based estrogens (phytoestrogens) like soy.
