Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FitForHer collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and contact information.
  • Health Information: Such as your age, weight, height, activity level, and health conditions.
  • Usage Data: Such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition plans and recommendations.
  • Improve the website and its services.
  • Communicate with you about updates and promotions.
  • Respond to your inquiries.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We will not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal requirements.
  • To protect our rights or the rights of others.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on the website. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

The FitForHer website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites.

7. Children's Privacy

The FitForHer website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at info@fitforher.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

FitForHer reserves the right to modify or revise this Privacy Policy at any time without notice. Your continued use of the website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@fitforher.com.
